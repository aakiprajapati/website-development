Who I am

Hi, I’m Aaki Prajapati , and design has been a part of me long before I even knew what the word meant. What started as curious play slowly grew into a passion for creating visuals that feel alive, personal, and memorable. Like the turning of seasons, design for me is about embracing change and creating beauty in it. It has always been a part of me, something that I’ve always loved doing. I like to think of my designs as little windows into moments: personal, authentic, and rooted in emotion. Today, I carry that same curiosity into my work. Whether it’s layouts, illustrations, or creative concepts, I always aim to create pieces that feel authentic, thoughtful, and human.

One more thing

Design, to me, is more than visuals, it is a language that tells stories and sparks connections. Every project is a new opportunity to explore creativity while learning and evolving along the way.
