Seasonal Veggie Soup at Mildreds, Soho

For those seeking a lighter yet equally satisfying option, Mildreds in Soho is the go-to spot for London’s best vegetarian cuisine. Their seasonal veggie soup changes with the harvest, meaning no two bowls taste the same. Whether it’s a carrot and ginger winter warmer or a spring pea and mint delight, each spoonful reflects fresh, organic ingredients sourced from local farms. Paired with warm sourdough and plant-based butter, the dish feels both nourishing and indulgent. The restaurant’s colorful, artistic interior makes it a favorite among creative Londoners.
Location: 45 Lexington St, London W1F 9AN
